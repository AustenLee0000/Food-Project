Sweet Osthumas
Lotus Root
桂花糖藕
Sweet osmanthus sugar lotus root is a Jiangnan region traditional snack, and autumn is the best season to eat lotus root, and the sweet osmanthus at this time full branches.
Lotus root is slightly sweet and crisp. It can be eaten raw or cooked. Moreover, it has high medicinal value. Lotus root originated from India and later introduced into China. It is now cultivated in shandong, henan, hebei and other places in China.
Ingredients
You will need
  • 80g Glutinous Rice

  • 2 sections of Lotus Roots

  • 60g Rock Sugar

  • 50g Brown Sugar

  • A handful of Sweet Osmanthus
